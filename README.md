# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by subscription-based businesses. This project analyzes customer behavior, identifies the key drivers of churn, and evaluates its impact on business revenue.

The project follows a complete Data Analytics workflow using **Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI**, starting from raw data exploration and database creation to advanced analysis, dashboard development, and business recommendations.

The objective is to help businesses understand customer attrition patterns, improve retention strategies, and minimize revenue loss.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

* Identify the factors influencing customer churn.
* Analyze customer demographics, contract types, services, and payment behavior.
* Measure the revenue impact of customer churn.
* Provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention and business performance.

# 🔍 Key Insights

### Contract Type

Customers on Month-to-Month contracts exhibit the highest churn rates compared to customers on annual and multi-year contracts.

### Customer Tenure

Most churn occurs within the first year, making customer onboarding and early engagement critical for retention.

### Internet Service

Fiber Optic customers generate substantial revenue but also demonstrate the highest churn behavior.

### Support Services

Customers without Tech Support, Online Security, and Device Protection are significantly more likely to churn.

### Payment Method

Customers using Electronic Check show higher churn rates compared to customers using automatic payment methods.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

### Strengthen Retention Programs

Encourage customers to switch from Month-to-Month plans to long-term contracts through loyalty rewards and promotional offers.

### Improve Early Customer Experience

Invest in onboarding and engagement programs during the first year of the customer lifecycle.

### Investigate Fiber Optic Churn

Analyze pricing, service quality, and customer feedback to improve retention among Fiber Optic subscribers.

### Promote Value-Added Services

Increase adoption of Tech Support, Online Security, and Device Protection through bundled service offerings.

### Encourage Automatic Payments

Promote automatic payment methods to improve customer commitment and reduce churn risk.

# 🚀 Project Outcome

This project demonstrates how data analytics can be leveraged to understand customer behavior, identify churn drivers, quantify revenue impact, and support business decision-making.

By focusing on customer retention during the early stages of the customer lifecycle, promoting long-term contracts, and increasing adoption of support-related services, businesses can significantly reduce churn and protect long-term revenue.
